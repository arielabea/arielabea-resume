I am a current UCR student in my first-year. I plan to obtain my degree in the School of Business with a Bachelor's in Business Administration or Marketing. I am a full-time commuting student while also working part-time.

Working in the food industry has been an enriching and transformative experience for me. Over the years, I have had the privilege of working at renowned establishments such as Raising Cane's, Chipotle Mexican Grill, and Five Guys Burger and Fries. Each of these establishments offered a unique insight into the industry, allowing me to develop essential skills, encounter diverse challenges, and grow both personally and professionally. Raising Cane's: The Art of Simplicity Raising Cane's, with its simple yet delectable menu of chicken fingers, swiftly became a hub of learning and growth for me. As a team member, I acquired valuable skills in customer service, order management, and food preparation. The fast-paced environment taught me to thrive under pressure while maintaining a positive and friendly attitude. Working alongside a dedicated team, I discovered the importance of collaboration and effective communication, ensuring the seamless operation of the restaurant. Moreover, I gained an appreciation for the significance of quality ingredients and consistent taste in providing an exceptional dining experience. Chipotle Mexican Grill: Unleashing Creativity Transitioning to Chipotle Mexican Grill, I found myself immersed in a vibrant atmosphere where customization and freshness were the key pillars. As a crew member, I honed my multitasking abilities, effortlessly maneuvering between tasks such as ingredient preparation, line assembly, and cashier duties. This fast-casual environment encouraged me to explore my creativity by constructing personalized meals for customers, carefully balancing flavors and textures. I learned the art of efficiently managing resources while adhering to the company's sustainability practices, promoting environmentally conscious choices. Chipotle's emphasis on food with integrity taught me the importance of responsible sourcing and the impact it has on both the environment and consumer satisfaction. Five Guys Burger and Fries: Pursuing Excellence My journey through the food industry culminated in my experience at Five Guys Burger and Fries, renowned for its made-to-order burgers and fresh-cut fries. Here, I embraced a meticulous approach to food preparation, ensuring every burger was assembled to perfection. The open kitchen concept allowed me to witness firsthand the dedication and attention to detail required in creating an exceptional dining experience. Moreover, I embraced the fast-paced environment, striving to deliver prompt and efficient service while maintaining high standards of quality. The emphasis on cleanliness and hygiene further ingrained in me the significance of maintaining a pristine work environment and adhering to rigorous food safety standards. Conclusion: My work experience at Raising Cane's, Chipotle Mexican Grill, and Five Guys Burger and Fries has been nothing short of transformative. From the art of simplicity at Raising Cane's to the unleashing of creativity at Chipotle and the pursuit of excellence at Five Guys, each establishment offered unique lessons and opportunities for growth. Through these experiences, I have acquired valuable skills in customer service, teamwork, multitasking, and attention to detail. Furthermore, I have gained a profound understanding of the importance of quality ingredients, customization, sustainability, and adherence to food safety standards. The food industry has not only nurtured my passion for culinary arts but has also instilled in me a strong work ethic and a commitment to delivering exceptional dining experiences. With the lessons learned from these establishments, I am excited to embark on new endeavors within the ever-evolving world of food and hospitality.
